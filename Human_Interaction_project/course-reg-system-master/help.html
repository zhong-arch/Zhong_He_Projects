Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Where I can search the courses I want register?

A: Go to add courses, register course, select term then search the courses.

Q: Where I can drop the courses?

A: Go to add courses, drop course, select term then drag that course that you want drop.

Q: Can I change my password?

A: Yes, go to Account, change my password.

Q: Can I change my address?

A: Yes, go to My Account, change my address.

Q: Can I pay my tuition fee online?

A; Yes, go to My Account, my tuition then select payment.

Q: Can I see my credit?

A: Yes, go to My Account, see my old credit.

Q: Where I can find my schedule?

A: Click My schedule.

Q: Can I see my exams in my schedule?

A: Yes, select the exam weeks, exam schedule will show on that week.
